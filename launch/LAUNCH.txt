MeepMap — how to run (Windows)
================================

1. Extract this entire folder anywhere you like (Desktop, Documents, etc.).
2. Double-click "Start MeepMap".

That's it. You do not need Node, npm, or a terminal.

Optional: double-click "Pin MeepMap to Desktop" to add a shortcut.

Tips
----
- MeepMap runs in the background. Look for the MeepMap icon in the system tray
  (near the clock). Right-click it to open the window or quit.
- If "Start MeepMap" says the app is missing, re-extract the full zip or ask
  for a fresh copy — do not move only the shortcut file by itself.
